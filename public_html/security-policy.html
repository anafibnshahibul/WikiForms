🛡️ Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

As an open-source tool built for the Wikimedia community, WikiForms takes security very seriously. If you believe you have found a security vulnerability, please follow this policy to report it to us.

How to Report

Please send your security findings via email to: your-email@example.com
You can use our PGP key available at pgp-key.txt to encrypt your report.

Guidelines

  • Provide sufficient details and steps to reproduce the vulnerability.
  • Give us a reasonable amount of time to fix the issue before making it public.
  • Do not delete or modify any data on the Toolforge server during your testing.
  • Do not perform DDoS attacks or social engineering against users.

Our Commitment

If you follow these guidelines, we promise to acknowledge your report within 48 hours, keep you updated on the fix, and add your name to our Hall of Fame.
